Common Questions

FAQs

How long does an inspection take?
Most inspections take 3-4 hours depending on the size and age of the home. We never rush — thoroughness is the whole point.
Should I attend the inspection?
Absolutely. We encourage you to be there for the last hour so we can walk through findings together in person.
When will I get my report?
Same day in most cases. Always within 24 hours. Digital format you can share with your agent immediately.
What areas do you serve?
All of the Triangle — Raleigh, Durham, Cary, Apex, Chapel Hill, Wendell, Wake Forest, Holly Springs, and surrounding areas.
What does the inspection cover?
Roof, attic, exterior, foundation, crawlspace, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, interior, insulation, ventilation, and appliances.
How much does an inspection cost?
Pricing depends on the home's size and age. Contact us for a quick quote — we're always transparent about costs upfront.
